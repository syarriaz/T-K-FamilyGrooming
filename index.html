About Us

Welcome to T&K Family Grooming, where we offer a range of pet grooming services to keep your furry squeaky clean.

T&K Family Grooming was founded in 2023. Named after Tyler and Khali-Bear, the family fur kids. Our love for pets goes beyond just a business; it's a genuine passion that runs deep within our family. We understand that pets are not just animals; they are beloved members of your family, bringing joy, comfort, and unconditional love into your lives.

At T&K Family Grooming, we treat your furry friends as if they were our own. We believe in creating a warm and welcoming environment where your pets can feel safe and pampered. Our skilled and caring team is dedicated to ensuring that every grooming session is not only about hygiene but also about making your pets look and feel their absolute best.

We cherish the trust you place in us to care for your pets, and we promise to repay that trust with the utmost dedication and affection. When you choose T&K Family Grooming, you're not just choosing a grooming service; you're becoming a part of our extended family. Thank you for letting us be a part of your pet's journey, and here's to many more years of fluffy cuddles and wagging tails!
